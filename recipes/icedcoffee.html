Iced Coffee Recipe

What's better than a winter's drink but in the summer?

What's better than constructing your desires at home!?

Description

Since the dishes on this website are made with so much love (and calories!!). Let's make a drink that's caloric-conscious and easy to make at home!

What's great about making iced coffee is: it's an opportunity to figure out exactly how you would like your caffeinated drink without breaking the bank.

A Toast! Let's raise our cold brewskies to the broskies (and girlskies and folkskies) that can't start their morning without their coffee!

What's Needed?

Ingredients

  • Coffee of your choosing
    • For this recipe, instant coffee will be used. Try and ensure your instant coffee's ingredients list does not contain chicory for a fuller experience.
      • (Chicory does have its nutritional perks, and may affect the taste).
  • Water
    • Hot and Cold
    • Liquid and Solid
  • (Optional): Sugar or sweetner of choosing
  • (Optional): Milk and/or cream of your choosing

Utensils

  • 2 - 3 Ice Trays
  • Cup or glass of your choosing
  • Kettle

How to:

  1. Ice Block prep

    1. Add cold water into an ice tray and store in the freezer.

  2. Coffee-Ice Block prep

    1. In a cup, prepare the dry ingredients for a cup of instant coffee.
      • If you're using sweetner or any syrups then add this too.
    2. Now add 10 mls of boiled water from the kettle into the cup.
    3. Pick up the cup and swirl to cause a whirlpool.
      • This should allow for the ingredients to dissolve. Using a teaspoon to stir works too.
    4. Using cold- or room temperature water fill the cup and stir.
    5. If the temperature of the coffee is at room temperature or colder, decant this coffee into the remaining iced trays and store in the freezer.
      • Repeat steps 1 - 3 to make coffee-ice block in stock. Making batches saves time downstream.

  3. Making the Iced Brew

    1. Once the ice - and coffee-ice blocks are formed we can making our brew!
    2. Start off by doing steps 1 - 4 from the "2. Coffee-Ice Block Prep". (When you get to step 4, don't completely fill the cup.)
      • Here's a recap:
        1. In a cup, prepare the dry ingredients for a cup of instant coffee.
          • If you're using sweetner or any syrups then add this too.
        2. Now add 10 mls of boiled water from the kettle into the cup.
        3. Pick up the cup and swirl to cause a whirlpool.
          • This should allow for the ingredients to dissolve. Using a teaspoon to stir works too.
        4. Using cold- or room temperature water fill (fill between 1/2-way - 3/4-way, depending on your milk/cream preferences and how strong you want the brew.) the cup and stir.
    3. Add 2 - 3 of your coffee-iced blocks
      • This will prevent the dilution in flavour as the "normal" ice melts.
    4. Fill cup with ice blocks if drinking milk - and cream free! Otherwise add ice blocks and whilst leaving room to add your remaining milk/cream of choice.
    5. Top up with sweetner/syrup if craving a sugary-kick!

In Closing...

Just like with the gatsby recipe, feel free to re-invent the wheel to your liking! Add tiny marshmellows, use vanilla ice cream. Let this inspire chaos and happiness!
